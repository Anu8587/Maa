About Us

Helping mothers,Impacting lives


Our Mission

At माँ, we are committed to empowering new mothers as part of our dedication to women empowerment. We understand the unique challenges and experiences that come with motherhood, and we are here to provide support, resources, and guidance every step of the way. We believe in the power of women and mothers to shape their own lives and futures. Through education, support, and advocacy, we empower new mothers to make informed decisions and achieve their goals. We foster a supportive and inclusive community where new mothers can connect, share experiences, and find solidarity. Together, we celebrate the joys of motherhood and navigate its challenges with strength and resilience.

Our Vision

At माँ, our vision goes beyond just empowering new mothers; it's about creating a world where every mother feels seen, heard, and valued. We understand that motherhood is a journey filled with highs and lows, triumphs and challenges, and we are here to walk alongside mothers every step of the way. Our Emotional Commitment Compassion: We approach our work with deep compassion and empathy, recognizing that each mother's journey is unique and deserving of respect. We listen with open hearts and offer support without judgment, knowing that sometimes a listening ear or a comforting word can make all the difference. Connection: We believe in the power of connection to heal, inspire, and uplift. Through our programs, events, and support groups, we create opportunities for mothers to connect with one another, forming bonds of friendship and solidarity that last a lifetime.
